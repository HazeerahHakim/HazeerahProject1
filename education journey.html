School Journey
JoyHouse Kindergarten
  • When I was 5 years old, I began my journey at JoyHouse Kindergarten, a place filled with joy and wonder. Those early years were some of the most exciting and carefree times of my life.
Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Melawati
  • At the age of 7 and 8, I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Melawati, where I began to truly explore the world of learning and discovery. These formative years were filled with exciting experiences as I transitioned from the basics of kindergarten to more structured academics.
Sekolah Kebangsaan Serendah
  • At the age of 9, I transferred to Sekolah Kebangsaan Serendah due to family matters. Although the transition was initially challenging, it became a significant chapter of my life. I spent my remaining primary school years there, from age 9 to 12, and completed my journey of primary education at this school.
Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Serendah
  • At the age of 13, I began my secondary school journey at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Serendah, where I studied during Form 1 and Form 2. It was a seamless transition, as many of my friends from Sekolah Kebangsaan Serendah joined me there. Reuniting with familiar faces made this period of my life even more enjoyable and comforting.
Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Dato Abu Bakar Baginda
  • At the age of 15 to 17, my secondary school journey continued at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Dato Abu Bakar Baginda in Bangi. This transfer happened because my father job required our family to relocate to Bangi. While leaving behind familiar surroundings and friends was challenging, it also marked a new chapter filled with opportunities for growth and learning. My school journey was ended up here.
Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)
  • After completing my primary and secondary school journey, I embarked on an exciting new chapter at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Rembau. Joining UiTM marked the beginning of my higher education journey, where I pursued a diploma in Information Management.
